# 🚀 AscendFit - Fitness RPG Mobile App

**AscendFit** is a comprehensive fitness RPG mobile application that transforms workouts into an engaging game experience. Designed for users aged 16+, it combines personal fitness tracking, competitive multiplayer battles, and AI-powered training plans into a single futuristic ecosystem.

---

## 🎮 Core Features

### **Three Main Modes**

#### 1. **Normal Mode** - Personal Fitness Progress
- **Z-A Rank System**: Progress through 26 ranks from Z (beginner) to A (advanced)
- **Daily Quests**: Earn +20 XP for completing daily workouts
- **Extra Quests**: Earn +10 XP for additional challenges
- **Penalty System**: Skip a quest and lose -20 XP; reach 0 XP to trigger a 2× difficulty penalty quest
- **Difficulty Scaling**: +2 reps per level, +5 reps per rank
- **Weekly/Monthly Quests**: Unlock badges, titles, and cosmetic rewards

#### 2. **Game Mode** - Competitive Fitness Battles
- **6 Game Types**:
  - 🐉 Solo Boss Battles
  - ⚔️ 1v1 Matches
  - 👥 Dual Co-op Battles
  - 🛡️ Team Raids
  - 🏰 Clan Wars
  - 👑 World Boss Events

- **Battle Mechanics**:
  - Reps = Damage against opponents/bosses
  - Perfect form = Critical hits (2× damage)
  - Combos = Damage multipliers
  - Real-time health bars and stamina tracking

- **Advanced Rank System**:
  - Basic Ranks: Z → A (26 ranks)
  - Advanced Ranks: Iron, Steel, Titan, Apex
  - Competitive Leagues: Bronze I-X through Grandmaster I-X
  - Seasonal rankings and divisions

#### 3. **Plan Mode** - AI Personal Trainer
- **Personalized Plans**: Based on goal, experience level, available days
- **Progressive Overload**: Automatic rep/weight increases
- **Deload Weeks**: Recovery phases every 4 weeks
- **Injury-Safe Alternatives**: Customizable modifications
- **Weekly Schedules**: Organized by muscle group and focus

## 🏆 Advanced Systems

### **Rank System**
```
Z → A (26 Basic Ranks)
  ↓
Iron, Steel, Titan, Apex (4 Advanced Ranks)
  ↓
Bronze I-X → Silver I-X → Gold I-X → Platinum I-X
  → Heroic I-X → Master I-X → Elite Master I-X → Grandmaster I-X
```

### **Points & Progression**
- Daily Quest: +20 XP
- Extra Quest: +10 XP
- Skip Quest: -20 XP
- Penalty Quest: 2× difficulty, earn double XP on completion
- 1000 XP = 1 Rank Up
- Reps scale: 10 + (Rank × 10)

### **Clan System**
- Create or join clans
- Clan XP and leveling
- Clan wars with score tracking
- Clan leaderboards
- Member roles (Leader, Officer, Member)
- Clan chat and collaboration

### **Progress Tracking**
- Weight tracking with BMI calculation
- Body measurements (chest, waist, arms, legs)
- Progress photos (front, side, back)
- Streak tracking (current, longest, total workouts)
- XP history and rank progression timeline
- Strength graphs and visual comparisons

## 🎮 Battle Mechanics

### **Damage Calculation**
```
Base Damage = 20-50
Critical Hit = Base × 2 (perfect form)
Combo Multiplier = 1 + (combo_count × 0.1)
Final Damage = Base × Critical × Combo
```

### **Boss Types**
1. **Inferno Dragon** - Level 15, 1000 HP
2. **Frozen Titan** - Level 18, 1200 HP
3. **Shadow King** - Level 20, 1500 HP
4. **Void Beast** - Level 22, 2000 HP
5. **Chaos God** - Level 25, 3000 HP
